Pancakes

Ingredient Quantity
Flour 2 cups
Eggs 2
Milk 1 cup

Instructions

  1. Mix all ingredients together.
  2. Heat a pan and pour the batter.
  3. Cook until golden brown.

Spaghetti Bolognese

Ingredient Quantity
Spaghetti 200g
Ground Beef 300g
Tomato Sauce 1 cup

Instructions

  1. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions.
  2. Brown the beef in a pan and add the tomato sauce.
  3. Combine the spaghetti with the sauce and serve.
